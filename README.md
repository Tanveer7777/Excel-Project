Blinkit Sales Performance Analysis Dashboard

➠Project Overview

This project analyzes Blinkit sales performance across multiple business factors such as:
Fat Content
Outlet Size
Outlet Location
Item Type
Outlet Establishment Year
The dashboard provides insights into sales distribution and performance trends using interactive visualizations.

➠Objectives

Identify which fat category contributes most to sales
Analyze sales distribution by item type
Compare performance across outlet sizes
Understand location-based sales differences
Track sales trends by outlet establishment year

➠Dashboard Insights
1. Total Sales by Fat Content
Low Fat: 65%
Regular: 35%
Low-fat products dominate total sales.

2. Total Sales by Item Type
Top-performing categories:
Fruits and Vegetables
Snack Foods
Household
Health and Hygiene

3. Sales by Outlet Size
Medium: 42% (Highest contributor)
Small: 37%
High: 21%
Medium outlets generate maximum revenue.

4. Sales by Outlet Location
Tier 3 cities show highest sales
Followed by Tier 2
Tier 1 contributes least

5. Total Sales by Outlet Establishment
Peak sales observed in earlier established outlets
Slight dip around 2011
Stable growth trend after 2012
